# Safety notes

## Laser (Class 3R/3B)
- OD ≥ 4 eyewear at 520–532 nm ON before laser power.
- Beam enclosed (foam-core), terminated on beam trap, propagating along bench away from door/eye level.
- Alignment at minimum power. No reflective jewelry/watches. Room access controlled during operation.

## RF
- PA output only into antenna or 50 Ω load — never open SMA at full power.
